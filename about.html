About Me

Hi! My name is Guoyang Luan. I am currently pursuing my bachelor degree in computer science in UBC. My background before I came to UBC for the CS degree was economics and finance. I did my previous bachelor degree in Economics and a master degree in Taxation in China and sequentially worked as an analyst in investment banking for two years. It was exactly where I learned to do things “smartly” mostly by taking advantage of data analysis techniques and script programming skills.

Specifically, I managed to write code to incorporate the raw market data automatically into weekly analysis reports so as to improve data analysis efficiency. It was so hard to build those script initially but once you get the hang of it, life became easy and fantastic. Gradually my interest in computer science got deeper and deeper so I decided to make a slight shift in my career goal which is to become a developer (ideally more finance-oriented).

In UBC, I got the change to fully grasp the knowledge of computer science systematically and comprehensively. I engaged in building interesting personal projects as well as participating in “hackathon” with initiative. I am so passionate and happy about what I am doing now. But I know that getting exposure to cutting-edge technologies and finding a position in those amazing tech companies require much more than what I’ve done so far.
